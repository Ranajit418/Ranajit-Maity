Women's Day- 13.03.2019:-

A small initiative by GMIT on the eve of Women's Day to educate people about women, the real architects of society. The event followed by cake cutting ceremony and Quiz competition at the end in the presence of our Vice Chairman, Mr. Bodhisattva Banerjee.

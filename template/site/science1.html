SCIENCE CARNIVAL- 06.10.2018:-

Rhode Island Public School (RIPS) in association with Gargi Memorial Institute of Technology (GMIT) organised a "Science Carnival" on 6th October 2018 at GMIT campus. The event was an effort to promote innovation, spread awareness on current trends and scientific growth through an exciting competition. Young students not only from GMIT & RIPS but also from various neighbouring schools of Baruipur specially S 24 parganas were given scope to showcasetheir talents in various categories like Science Quiz, Model Competition, Poster presentation, Photography competition and Cultural events. It is an exhibition of creativity and fun.
